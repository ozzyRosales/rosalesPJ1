My journey through the vast expanse of the internet began in 2006, a milestone marked by the gift of my first computer, a Dell Dimension e510, from my parents on my birthday. Since then, my online presence has evolved alongside me, becoming an integral part of my life. Over the years, my internet explorations have expanded to encompass a wide array of interests, with a particular focus on design inspiration and technology. I find immense joy in the intersection of these two realms, marveling at the innovative ideas and projects that emerge from their fusion. Instagram has become my primary source of inspiration, its algorithm finely tuned to deliver content tailored to my preferences. Yet, when the urge to explore new perspectives strikes, I turn to Twitter, deliberately seeking out diverse voices and viewpoints to broaden my horizons.
